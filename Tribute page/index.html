A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man Of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam 15 October 1931- 27 July 2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and Studied physics and aerospace engineering.He spent the next four decades as a scientist and science administrator, mainly at the Defenece Reasearch and Oraganisation (IRSO) and was intimately involved in Indians civilian space programme and military missile developement efforts. came to known as the Missile man of India for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vechicle technology,played a pivotal organisational, technical, and political role in Indias Pokhran-2 nuclear tests in 1998 , the first since the orignal nuclear test by India in 1974.
